Need practice braille typing?

Thank you for visiting My Brailler. This site lets you practice braille typing in the most efficient manner.

My Brailler is dedicated to typing beginners and intermediates;
It reads aloud every single letter you type. So you don't need to reach out the braille paper just to know what you typed.

Enjoy!

Keyboard layout and braille font

  • F key: Dot 1
  • D key: Dot 2
  • S key: Dot 3
  • J key: Dot 4
  • K key: Dot 5
  • L key: Dot 6
  • delete key: Delete previous dots
  • enter key: Delete a full line (Enter the answer in exercise page)
  • alt key: View cheatsheet (Available in free typing page)
  • space key: Insert a space
